Summary

An exquisite over-and-under shotgun that combines Italian craftsmanship with modern engineering, catering to discerning shooters who appreciate both performance and aesthetics. Caesar Guerini is well-regarded for producing high-quality shotguns, and the Apex is no exception.

The Apex  is characterised by its elegant design and meticulous attention to detail. The walnut stock is carefully selected for its quality and beauty, featuring fine checkering that not only enhances the grip but also adds a touch of refinement. The stock is expertly shaped to provide a comfortable and natural feel for the shooter.

Details

The receiver of the Apex is adorned with detailed engraving, showcasing intricate scrollwork. This engraving is done by skilled artisans, making each Apex shotgun a unique piece of functional art. The attention to aesthetics extends to every part of the firearm, contributing to its overall visual appeal.

Performance

Functionally, the Apex is designed to deliver top-notch performance. The barrel provides a balance between power and reduced recoil, making it versatile for various shooting applications. 

The Apex is equipped with the DTS (Dynamic Tuning System) trigger system, offering a crisp and responsive pull. This enhances the shooter's ability to achieve consistent and accurate shots. The gun's overall balance and handling characteristics are carefully considered to provide an enjoyable and effective shooting experience.
